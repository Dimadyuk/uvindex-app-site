Analytics

The Application uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to collect anonymized usage statistics. Firebase Analytics automatically collects the following information:

  • Device identifiers (Firebase installation ID)

  • Country and approximate region
  • Session duration and frequency of use

This data is used solely to understand how users interact with the Application and to improve its functionality. Firebase Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information and does not link collected data to individual users.

Data collected via Firebase Analytics is processed by Google LLC in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Firebase Analytics data collection at any time through your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization, or by uninstalling the Application.
